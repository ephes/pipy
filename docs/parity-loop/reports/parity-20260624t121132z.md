## Comprehension Check

1. Why does `docs/terminal-setup.md` say pipy does not use the alternate screen?

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

Pipy's product TUI is inline: finalized output is committed once to normal
terminal scrollback, while only the bottom live region is repainted. That means
native terminal or tmux scrollback keeps working.
</details>

2. What tmux setting format does the new tmux page recommend for modified Enter
   keys?

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

For tmux 3.4 or newer it recommends extended keys with CSI-u:
`set -g extended-keys always` and `set -g extended-keys-format csi-u`, plus the
`terminal-features` extkeys line.
</details>
